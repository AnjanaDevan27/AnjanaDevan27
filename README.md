# Hey, I'm Anjana! ✨

I recently graduated from Northeastern University with a Masters in Data Science. Grew up across 3 countries and somewhere along the way, data became the thing that made the most sense to me.

I get excited when data finally clicks and tells a story. Right now I am deep in LLMs, RAG pipelines, and AI systems, while slowly bridging into data engineering to understand the full picture of how data moves, not just what it means.

My interests sit at the intersection of AI and real world impact, building intelligent systems that actually solve problems, not just demonstrate concepts. I am curious about AI agents, prompt engineering, and how LLMs can be grounded in data to make better decisions. On the engineering side, I enjoy thinking about how data pipelines are designed, how systems scale, and how everything connects from raw data to insight.
